Privacy Policy for Simple Sticky Notes

Last Updated: 27-11-25

Your privacy is important. This extension, Simple Sticky Notes, does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.

Information Collection

The extension does not collect any personal information.

All notes are saved only on your browser using localStorage.

No data is uploaded to any server.

Data Usage

Notes are used only inside your browser.

The extension does not send any data to external websites or APIs.

Data Sharing

No data is shared with third parties.

No analytics, no ads, no tracking.

Permissions

No special permissions are used other than localStorage.

The extension does not access browsing history, tabs, cookies, or websites.

Security

All data stays on your device.

You can delete your notes anytime.
